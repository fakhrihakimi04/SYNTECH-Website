ABOUT US

FOUNDED IN 2011
By Dr. Ahmad Sobri

We are SYNTECH, a technology club that aims to provide our members with the necessary knowledge to use the latest and greatest software development tools. We also pursue tangent projects like community service projects to provide our members with a holistic skill development opportunity. With a slogan 'Synergy Through Technology' and our Motto is 'Think, Train, Innovate, Compete'. We strive to achive tier 1 and maintain as the best technology club in UTP
